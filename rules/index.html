Time Management

Your time is precious and you should be cautious about how you spend it. As Hamming pointed out in his famous talk “You and Your Research”, opportunities favor minds that are well prepared. You should always ask yourself: Am I well prepared for something? If not, how far are your from that? What are required for you to reach that? Answers to these questions will motivate you to spend your time more wisely.
